# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all participants in the Control Room community to uphold this Code of Conduct.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Intimidation, threats, or violent behavior

## Enforcement

Violations of this code may result in:

1. Warning - First-time offenders receive a warning
2. Temporary Ban - Repeated violations may result in temporary restrictions
3. Permanent Ban - Serious or repeated violations result in permanent removal

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All GitHub repositories under mcp-tool-shop organization
- Issue comments and pull request discussions
- Community forums and chat channels
- In-person events and conferences

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation:

1. **Document the incident** - Note what happened, when, and who was involved
2. **Report privately** - Email maintainers with details
3. **Include context** - Links to comments, screenshots, or other evidence

Reports will be reviewed confidentially and action taken as appropriate.
